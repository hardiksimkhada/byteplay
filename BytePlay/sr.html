How to Play

There are three vertical traffic lights:

- First, the 1st traffic light turns all 3 red bulbs on.
- Then the 2nd traffic light turns all 3 red bulbs on.
- Then the 3rd traffic light turns all 3 red bulbs on.

Finally, all three traffic lights turn green simultaneously.
When the green lights appear, tap/click anywhere or press spacebar to stop the timer.

Try to beat the world record of 0.04 seconds! Good luck!
